Alerts

Milan Bergamo Airport strongly recommends passengers to stay updated on the status of their flight, through the official channels of the airline or on the homepage of our website www.milanbergamoairport.it

In the event of flight cancellation, the airport invites to take advantage of the means of re-routing made available online by the airlines, facilitating their operations and the activities of the terminal.

You can change your booking free of charge or request a refund by logging into your accounts or by contacting the carrier's Call Center.

Refunds cannot be made at the airport but will only be re-credited using the payment method made for the booking.
